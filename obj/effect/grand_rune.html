grand_rune
/obj/effect/grand_rune

Note: this is very cool and also automatically ends the round with a cutscene after about three minutes. This follows the at least 3 minutes of warning they have had about it happening. Should be a rare event, so I guess we will see how much people hate it.

Magic rune used in the grand ritual. A wizard sits themselves on this thing and waves their hands for a while shouting silly words. Then something (usually bad) happens.
